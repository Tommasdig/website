About Me

Tommaso Digre

Innovative Furniture Designer and Restoration Specialist with over 10 years of experience spanning industrial design, interior spaces, and furniture craftsmanship.

Uniquely combining formal design education from prestigious international institutions with hands-on woodworking expertise gained through three years of furniture restoration.
